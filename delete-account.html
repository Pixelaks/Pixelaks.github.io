Account Deletion Request

Adhyora is an Institutional Management System designed for Colleges. Because your academic records (Attendance, Marks, Enrollments) are legal documents owned by your Institution, individual users cannot unilaterally delete their data without administrative approval.

Data Deletion Timeline: Once authorized by the College, your personal data will be permanently removed from our active databases within 30 days.

For Students & Teachers

Your account is managed directly by your College Administration. To request deletion:

  1. Contact your Head of Department (HOD) or the College Principal.
  2. Request them to "Remove User" from the Adhyora Dashboard.
  3. Once they perform this action, your login access is revoked immediately, and data deletion begins.

For Principals / Admins

If you wish to terminate your entire Institution's account with Adhyora, please contact us directly.

You can email us at support@pixelaks.in or use the contact form to initiate the termination process.

Can't Contact your College?

If you have left the institution and they are refusing to remove your personal data, or if the institution is no longer active, we can help.

Please Click Here to Open a Support Ticket.

In your message, please provide:

  • Name of the College
  • Your Roll Number / Staff ID
  • Reason for deletion request

We will verify your identity and contact the institution on your behalf to process the deletion.
