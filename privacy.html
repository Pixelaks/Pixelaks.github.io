Privacy Policy for Adhyora

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Pixelaks Technologies ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information through the Adhyora Academic Management System. Adhyora is a platform designed to centralize academic administration for educational institutions. By using Adhyora, the institution and its users agree to the data handling practices described in this policy.

2. Relationship Disclosure

Pixelaks Technologies acts as a Data Processor. The educational institution (the "Institution") using Adhyora acts as the Data Fiduciary and retains primary ownership and control over all academic data entered into the system.

3. Information Collection and Roles

We collect and process information based on user roles within the Institution:

  • Principal & Administrative Data: We collect contact details and administrative logs to manage institutional structures, departments, and user onboarding.
  • Faculty/Teacher Data: We process names, department associations, subject assignments, and teaching schedules.
  • Student Data: We process names, department/year associations, unique institutional IDs, attendance records, and internal marks.
  • Academic Content: We host and distribute assignment descriptions, timetables, and institutional calendars.

4. Purpose of Data Processing

Pixelaks Technologies processes this data solely to provide the Adhyora service:

  • Academic Tracking: Calculating attendance percentages and Continuous Evaluation (CE) marks.
  • Communication: Sending instant assignment notifications and college updates to students.
  • Administrative Control: Enabling Principals to monitor faculty activity and student performance with complete visibility.
  • Data Portability: Allowing authorized users to export academic records for official institutional reporting.

5. Data Security and Infrastructure

Pixelaks Technologies employs industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

  • Storage: We utilize Google Firebase for secure cloud-based data storage and authentication.
  • Access Control: The Principal/Admin has the final authority to approve, revoke, or remove user access.
  • Disclaimer: While we strive for 100% accuracy, Pixelaks Technologies is not responsible for administrative decisions or academic outcomes based on user-entered data. Adhyora is provided "as-is".

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Academic records are retained only as long as the Institution maintains an active subscription or as required by law. Upon account deletion by the Institution's Administrator, all associated personal data is purged from our active systems.

7. Grievance Redressal

In accordance with the Information Technology Act and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), any grievances or questions regarding data handling by Pixelaks Technologies may be directed to our Grievance Officer:

Company: Pixelaks Technologies
Email: pixelaks.technologies@gmail.com
