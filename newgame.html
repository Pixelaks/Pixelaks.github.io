TypoTopple

The TypoTopple game was created by AksGames. “TypoTopple” Like the name there are many TypoTopple things in this game, And It’s really fun to play. How To Play This Game? Ride the UFO without hitting the obstacles

How to Play

There are two types of controls in this game, You can’t choose which control you want. So you wonder then why this two controls right? These controls depends on the level, In some levels you can see “Tap To Start”, so if you touch any side of the screen in that level, the UFO will go up.
In some other levels, you can see “UP” on the left side of the screen and “Down” on the right side of the screen, so if you touch the left side of the screen, the UFO will go up, and if you touch the right side, the UFO will go down.
And one More thing don’t fly the UFO too high or too low
