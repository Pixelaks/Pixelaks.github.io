Academic Management System

The Essence

"Adhyora is a modern name derived from the Sanskrit root Adhyayan, which means learning. Adhyora represents a platform that supports and manages the entire academic journey."

Adhyora is an integrated college management platform designed to simplify and centralize academic administration. Principals and administrators can manage student and teacher records, academic calendars, attendance, marks, and institutional activities with complete control and visibility. Teachers can efficiently manage department-wise student data, attendance, marks, timetables, and academic progress, while students access all their academic information, schedules, attendance, marks, and important college updates in one unified app.

Our Vision

We are driven by the dream to eliminate the old paper-based methods and fully digitalise all colleges in India. Adhyora is our commitment to building a faster, smarter, and more transparent future for educational institutions nationwide.

NEP & Traditional Support

Future-proof your institution. Adhyora supports both the New Education Policy (NEP) and Traditional Syllabus structures. Simply select the syllabus method your college follows, and the platform will exclusively configure itself to run on that specific academic standard.

Adaptive Attendance Logic

Every university calculates differently. Adhyora supports Multiple Calculation Modes—whether it's session-based, hour-based, or subject-specific percentage thresholds. We adapt to your rules, not the other way around.

Principal Control Panel

Institution & User Management

Principals can manage the complete institutional structure by onboarding students and teachers, assigning them to departments and years, and maintaining full visibility of active users. The platform provides authority to approve or revoke access for teachers and students at any time, ensuring secure and controlled usage.

Department Oversight & Faculty Control

Monitor department-wise faculty activity, view which teachers belong to which departments, and search teachers easily based on department association. Principals retain the power to review access permissions, remove faculty when required, and maintain a well-organized academic hierarchy.

Student Search & Academic Performance Tracking

Search students by name, department, or year of study and view detailed academic insights including semester-wise attendance percentages and internal marks. This enables principals to track individual and department-level performance efficiently.

Academic Calendar & Institutional Planning

Create, manage, and publish the official college academic calendar, keeping all students and faculty aligned with institutional schedules, events, and academic timelines from a single source of truth.

Institutional Data Access & Export

Download complete academic data including student records, attendance logs, and internal marks department-wise. This data can be securely stored, reviewed, or integrated into institutional reporting systems whenever required.

Educator Portal

Class & Student Management

Teachers can view students organized by department and year, define subjects for the classes they handle, and manage teaching responsibilities efficiently. Authorized access allows teachers to work with multiple departments when required.

Attendance Management

Mark student attendance department-wise and year-wise with real-time updates. Attendance can only be recorded on designated working days, ensuring accuracy and compliance with academic schedules defined at the department level.

Internal Marks & Academic Evaluation

Record internal exam marks, attendance marks, and assignment scores for each student. The system automatically calculates continuous evaluation (CE) marks, giving teachers and department heads a clear view of academic performance.

Assignments & Student Notifications

Create and publish assignments by selecting target years, adding topics, descriptions, and due dates. Students receive instant notifications, ensuring clear communication and timely academic engagement.

Timetable & Academic Calendar Access

View department timetables and the official college academic calendar. Department heads can manage year-wise timetables and define working or non-working days, which directly control attendance availability.

Search, Access Control & Data Export

Search students by name, department, or year to access detailed academic records including attendance percentages and marks. Teachers can download academic data year-wise for analysis and reporting.

Student Dashboard

Academic Overview & Performance

Students get a complete view of their academic progress, including attendance percentage, total classes conducted, classes attended, absences, and continuous evaluation (CE) marks in one place.

Detailed Attendance Insights

View period-wise attendance status to know whether a class was marked as present or absent. Students can also see when attendance was recorded and which teacher marked it.

Assignments, Timetable & Notifications

Receive assignment notifications with topics, descriptions, and due dates. Students can access their year-wise timetable and stay updated with class schedules easily.

College Calendar & Academic Updates

Stay informed with the official college academic calendar, including working days, holidays, and important academic events, helping students plan their studies effectively.

System Inquiries

Is Adhyora compatible with the new NEP structure?

Yes. We have built a dedicated NEP Logic that handles multi-disciplinary courses, flexible subject choices, so colleges can choose correct syllabus.

Can we export data for NAAC/Audit reports?

Absolutely. Principals and Admins can export comprehensive Excel reports on attendance, marks, and faculty performance with a single click.

Is the student data secure?

We use industry-standard encryption protocols. Data access is strictly role-based, meaning a student cannot access another student's data, and unauthorized access is blocked.

Setup is rapid. Once we receive your student and faculty lists, our team configures your instance within 48-72 hours.
